## Portland Datacenter (PDX-1)

- **Region**: us-west
- **Address**: 4520 NW Industrial Way, Portland, OR 97210
- **Operator**: Meridian Tech (owned facility)
- **Rack Range**: R-400 through R-499
- **Power**: 2 MW capacity, dual-feed utility power with diesel backup generators
- **Cooling**: Hot aisle / cold aisle containment, N+1 CRAC units
- **Network**: 100 Gbps backbone, dual upstream providers (Lumen, Zayo)
- **Certifications**: SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001
- **Commissioned**: 2021-03-15

The Portland datacenter serves as the primary facility for compute-intensive workloads. It was built to handle high-density deployments up to 30 kW per rack. The facility has on-site security staff 24/7 and biometric access controls for all server rooms.

## Chicago Datacenter (CHI-1)

- **Region**: us-central
- **Address**: 350 E Cermak Rd, Suite 400, Chicago, IL 60616
- **Operator**: Equinix (colocation)
- **Rack Range**: R-100 through R-199
- **Power**: 500 kW allocated, single-feed with UPS
- **Cooling**: Raised floor cooling, shared CRAC
- **Network**: 40 Gbps backbone, single upstream (Cogent)
- **Certifications**: SOC 2 Type II
- **Commissioned**: 2019-08-22

The Chicago datacenter is a colocation facility used primarily for internal tools and staging environments. It has lower power density and is not suitable for high-performance workloads.

## Ashburn Datacenter (IAD-1)

- **Region**: us-east
- **Address**: 21715 Filigree Ct, Ashburn, VA 20147
- **Operator**: QTS (colocation)
- **Rack Range**: R-200 through R-299
- **Power**: 1 MW allocated, dual-feed with UPS and generator
- **Cooling**: In-row cooling, hot aisle containment
- **Network**: 100 Gbps backbone, triple upstream (Lumen, Cogent, Telia)
- **Certifications**: SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, PCI DSS
- **Commissioned**: 2020-11-10

The Ashburn datacenter is the primary east coast facility, located in the Virginia data center corridor. It handles payment processing and other latency-sensitive workloads that need proximity to financial networks on the east coast.
